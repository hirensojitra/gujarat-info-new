Data Deletion Instructions

At Khodaldham Mobile, we respect your right to have your data deleted. Follow the steps below to request data deletion:

1. Contact Us

Send an email to gujaratuvach@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."

2. Verification

To ensure the request is legitimate, we may ask you to verify your identity. This is to protect your data from unauthorized access.

3. Processing

Once your request is verified, we will delete your data from our systems within 30 days.

Alternative Option

If the app provides a built-in data deletion feature, you can navigate to the “Settings” section in the app and click “Delete My Data.”
